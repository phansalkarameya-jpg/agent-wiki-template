# Metric Definitions

## CPA (Cost Per Acquisition)

**Definition:** Total channel spend divided by number of acquisitions (bookings) in the same period.
**How it's calculated:** Spend ÷ Bookings. Tracked via Adjust + GA4.

## CPA/LTV

**Definition:** CPA expressed as a ratio against customer lifetime value. Used to assess long-term channel efficiency.
**How it's calculated:** CPA ÷ LTV. LTV sourced from internal data.

## Conversion Rate

**Definition:** Percentage of users who complete a booking after clicking an ad or opening a CRM message.
**How it's calculated:** Bookings ÷ Clicks (or Opens for CRM). Tracked via Adjust / GA4.
