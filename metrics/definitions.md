# Metric Definitions

## CPA (Cost Per Acquisition)

**Definition:** Total channel spend divided by number of bookings in the same period.
**How it's calculated:** Spend ÷ Bookings. Tracked via Adjust + GA4.
**Current benchmark:** [To be updated from source data]

---

## CPA/LTV

**Definition:** CPA expressed as a ratio against customer lifetime value. Measures long-term channel efficiency.
**How it's calculated:** CPA ÷ LTV. LTV sourced from internal data.
**Current benchmark:** [To be updated from source data]

---

## Conversion Rate

**Definition:** Percentage of users who complete a booking after clicking an ad or opening a CRM message.
**How it's calculated:** Bookings ÷ Clicks (paid) or Opens (CRM). Tracked via Adjust / GA4.
**Current benchmark:** [To be updated from source data]

---

## Install Volume

**Definition:** Total number of app installs attributed to a channel in a given period.
**How it's calculated:** Tracked via Adjust.
**Current benchmark:** [To be updated from source data]

---

## CPI (Cost Per Install)

**Definition:** Total channel spend divided by installs attributed to that channel.
**How it's calculated:** Spend ÷ Installs. Tracked via Adjust.
**Current benchmark:** [To be updated from source data]

---

## Bookings

**Definition:** Total confirmed bookings driven by a channel or campaign.
**How it's calculated:** Attributed via Adjust (paid) and GA4 (organic/CRM).
**Current benchmark:** [To be updated from source data]

---

## YoY Growth

**Definition:** Year-over-year percentage change in a key metric (bookings, installs, or revenue).
**How it's calculated:** (Current period − Same period last year) ÷ Same period last year × 100.
**Current benchmark:** [To be updated from source data]

---

## NMV (Net Merchandise Value)

**Definition:** Net value of bookings driven by a campaign, after cancellations.
**How it's calculated:** Internal — sum of confirmed booking values minus cancellations.
**Known data point:** Low Util SNB test week of Mar 30 = $7,000 NMV (vs $2,560 week of Mar 23, rain-affected).

---

## WA Cost Per Message

**Definition:** Cost charged per WhatsApp message sent via CleverTap / Meta Business.
**Current rate:** USD 0.0499/message (since Jan 2026). Must factor into all WA campaign ROI calculations.
