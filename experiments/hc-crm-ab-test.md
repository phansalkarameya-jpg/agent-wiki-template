# HC CRM AB Test

**Channel:** CRM (Email, WhatsApp, Push)
**Platform:** CleverTap
**Attribution:** CleverTap internal

## Hypothesis

"1-hour free" messaging will outperform deal-based comms for active Home Care (HC) segments, improving conversion and reducing discount dependency.

## Mechanic

**Segment logic (finalised Apr 7):**
- All active segments (incl. New) → 50/50 split
  - 50% receive deal comms
  - 50% receive "1-hour free" comms
- Inactive / SNB / Keeta segments → no change (excluded from test)
- Low_util_snb → 10 AED direct send, no test

**Owner:** Emre creating the segment split. Sabhyata executing campaigns.
