# Low Utilisation SNB AB Test

**Channel:** CRM WhatsApp / Push
**Platform:** CleverTap
**Attribution:** Internal

## Hypothesis

Targeting low-utilisation SNB (Send Now/Buy) users with a specific offer will increase NMV and conversion rate vs the control.

## Mechanic

CRM campaign targeting low-utilisation SNB segment. 10 AED direct offer — no A/B split for this segment (decided: direct send, no test). Compared against baseline week.

## Results

| Metric | Week of Mar 23 | Week of Mar 30 | Change |
|---|---|---|---|
| NMV | $2,560 | $7,000 | +$4,440 (~+173%) |
| Conversion Rate | 0.1% | 0.23% | +0.13pp |

**Caveat:** Week of Mar 23 was rain-affected (~30% lower performance overall). Week of Mar 30 data is cleaner but one more week of data requested to validate incrementality properly.

## Next Step

Emre to check same hubs for one more week of data. If validated, decision to scale. Ameya to update AB tracker with results + rain caveat.

## Status

Monitoring — results promising but rain effect on Mar 23 week means one more week of clean data needed before scaling decision.
